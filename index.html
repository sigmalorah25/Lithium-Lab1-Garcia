Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is a free-to-play action-rpg available on Windows, Mac, IOS, Android and even on the Playstation. The game takes place in a fantasy world called "Teyvat". Teyvat is compromised of 7 nations namely: Mondstadt, Liyue, Inazuma, Sumeru, Fontain, Natlan, and Snezhnaya that is each ruled by 7 different archons (gods). These nations are tied to specific elements like cryo (ice), dendro (plants), pyro (fire), anemo(wind) and etc. The story starts off in a shore near Mondstadt. Where a traveler who was put to sleep by an unknown god for hundreds of years and meets a flying companion called Paimon. Now, they are off to travel to find the traveler's sibling through exploring the 7 nations mentioned. This game is also a gacha (gambling) game, where you can obtain your favorite characters and weapons through the gacha system.
